US Mass Shootings Over the Past 3 Years (2018-2021) 

Generally mass shootings are understood as incidents that shooters kill a large number of people using firearms.
However, there is no standard definition of what constitutes mass shootings.
In this site, I use the criteria that "a shooter kills at least four people" defined by Gun Violence Archive.
By this definition, unfortunately, mass shootings in the the US account for nearly one-third of the world’s mass shootings according to a study published in 2017.
